Resort Management System

Streamline your resort operations, secure your cash flow, and enhance your guest experience. Combines a powerful POS, real-time inventory tracking, and advanced reservation management into one intuitive platform.

Resort Management System Core Features

Smart POS & Billing

  • Flexible Tour Pricing (Day/Night rates).
  • Dynamic pricing for Regular, Child, Senior, & PWD.
  • Split Billing & separate Add-on receipts.
  • Auto-calculated stay extensions.

Reservation Calendar

  • Interactive visual booking calendar.
  • Downpayment tracking & auto-balance calculation.
  • Flexible rescheduling with optional fees.
  • Smart dashboard alerts for scheduled arrivals.

Real-Time Inventory

  • Automated stock deduction upon billing.
  • Pulsing low stock alerts for admins.
  • Categorized management (Services, Rentables, Consumables).
  • Barcode Scanner Ready for fast retail checkout.

Shift & Cash Mgmt

  • Shift Locking requiring float/petty cash declaration.
  • Mid-Shift X-Reading for real-time snapshots.
  • End-of-Day Z-Reading forces cash count.
  • Calculates perfect balances, shortages, and overages.

Analytics & Guest Data

  • Interactive charts for daily net revenue.
  • Comprehensive Guest History & Transaction Logs.
  • One-click Excel export for accounting.
  • Strict role-based access control (Admin vs. Cashier).

Hardware & Receipts

  • Dynamic White-label Receipts (Logo, VAT/TIN, Footer).
  • Thermal Printer Integration (USB & Network IP).
  • Automatic Cash Drawer Kick via ESC/POS.
  • Smart Multi-PC to Printer Routing over LAN.
